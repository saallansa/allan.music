Hello!

Thank you for visiting my website!

I'm sharing a bit of my musical journey through the following sections.

Symphonic Percussion

I graduated in Music with a concentration in Percussion Instrumental Studies. I have been a percussionist in the main symphony orchestras of Costa Rica and have performed with other orchestras abroad. I've also played in symphonic bands, percussion ensembles, and other chamber groups, performing a repertoire ranging from classical to contemporary pieces.

Popular Percussion

I have been part of traditional music groups from Costa Rica, representing the country in various places in America, Europe, and Asia, playing marimba, drums, congas or bongos. I joined a Brazilian Popular Music (MPB) trio, specializing in pandeiro and drums for samba and bossa nova. I studied drums for jazz, rock and Latin rhythms and have played for Ska, reggae and Latin music groups such as salsa, timba, merengue, cumbia and other genres.

Arrangements & Composition

I have written arrangements for symphonic and popular music groups such as percussion ensembles, folk ensembles and salsa orchestras, and I have composed music for dance groups. I work with industry-standard music notation softwares like Sibelius, Musescore or Finale.

Teaching & Pedagogy

I graduated in Music Education and worked as a professor of symphonic percussion in a conservatory in Costa Rica. I have taught theoretical and practical classes for percussion and drum instruments, as well as classes on reading, harmony, and other areas of musical language.

Music Production (Current Project)

I graduated in master in music creation and production that includes electronic and urban music, composition for cinema, composition and implementation of music for video games and song writing, with practices in areas such as sound design, recording, synthesis, mixing and audio mastering.
